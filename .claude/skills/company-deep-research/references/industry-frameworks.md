# Industry-Specific Analysis Frameworks

## Technology / Software

### SaaS Companies

**Key Metrics:**
- ARR / MRR growth rate
- Net Revenue Retention (NRR)
- CAC Payback period
- LTV/CAC ratio
- Rule of 40 (Growth % + FCF Margin %)
- Gross margin (target: >70%)

**Competitive Analysis:**
- Product differentiation
- Switching costs / lock-in
- Platform vs point solution
- AI integration capability
- Developer ecosystem

**Valuation Benchmarks:**
- EV/ARR: 5-15x (growth dependent)
- EV/Revenue: 3-20x
- Rule of 40 scoring

### Hardware / Semiconductors

**Key Metrics:**
- Gross margin trend
- R&D as % of revenue
- Inventory turnover
- Book-to-bill ratio
- Design wins pipeline

**Cycle Considerations:**
- Where in the semiconductor cycle?
- Inventory levels across supply chain
- Capacity utilization rates

## Financial Services

### Banks

**Key Metrics:**
- Net Interest Margin (NIM)
- Return on Assets (ROA)
- Return on Equity (ROE)
- Efficiency ratio
- NPL ratio (Non-Performing Loans)
- CET1 Capital ratio

**Risk Assessment:**
- Loan book composition
- Interest rate sensitivity
- Credit quality trends
- Deposit stability

### Asset Managers

**Key Metrics:**
- AUM growth (organic vs market)
- Fee rate / basis points
- Investment performance vs benchmark
- Flows (net new assets)

**Structural Considerations:**
- Active vs passive mix
- Fee pressure trends
- Distribution channels

---

## Consumer / Retail

### E-commerce

**Key Metrics:**
- GMV (Gross Merchandise Value)
- Take rate / monetization rate
- Customer acquisition cost
- Repeat purchase rate
- Average order value

**Competitive Moat:**
- Selection/assortment
- Fulfillment/logistics
- Customer loyalty
- Marketplace network effects

### Traditional Retail

**Key Metrics:**
- Same-store sales growth
- Comparable store sales
- Sales per square foot
- Inventory turnover
- Store productivity

**Structural Headwinds:**
- Online competition
- Real estate costs
- Labor costs
- Omnichannel capability

**Key Metrics:**
- Organic growth

## Framework Application

For any company analysis:

1. **Identify primary industry** and apply relevant framework
2. **Cross-check** with adjacent industries if applicable
3. **Adjust for company stage** (growth vs mature)
4. **Consider regional factors** (US/China/Europe/EM)
5. **Note structural changes** affecting the industry
